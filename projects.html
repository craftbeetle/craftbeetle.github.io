PROJECTS

I design and make things, be they physical objects or tech projects. Some of these projects are even available to download for free.

Coffee Pod Holder

I have a coffee maker at work but I prefer to clean and fill my coffee pods at home so my coffee grounds can go into my compost. So I take my coffee pods back and forth from work. The coffee pods sometimes spill during transport and are generally inconvenient to carry. So I designed a coffee pod holder that uses four holding plates with a threaded rod and nuts holding them together. When closed it clamps down on all the pods, and when I want to access a pod I just unscrew the plate above a few turns.
