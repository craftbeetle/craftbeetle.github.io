Simple Thoughts

This is my very simple first project. Indeed the website itself is the first project. I have finally learned HTML and CSS. This is a cool moment for me. Now, I am one step closer to the machine. The machine. That infinite creature we build and destroy. That creature we have been seeking all our lives. That creature that will replace us. For man seeks as once to live and not live. To be and not be. To do and not do. Human existance is defined by the good and the bad we experience, and the good and bad reactions to them. Yet man seeks to clense this and bring about heaven on earth. A unnatural state of perfection, purity, and static sameness. To achieve perfection is to die. Thus, heaven was locked away past the gates on death.
