GARDEN

The plants and fungi I have run across in my life. Some of them are at my own garden, some are in the community garden, and some are out in the wild.

I am trying to identify the genus and species of all the plants I have taken pictures. Some of them are relatively easy, while others have thousands of species that look nearly identical to each other so if you notice a mistake or if you have identified something I have missed please feel free to email me. I would love your input and help!
