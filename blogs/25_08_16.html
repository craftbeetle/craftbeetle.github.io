Long Live The Queen

25.08.16 BACK TO BLOGS

A monarch visited me as I sat on a swinging bench in my backyard.

My cat roamed and hunted for lizards and bugs.

The monarch landed on each leaf of the milkweed beside me and diligently laid a single egg.

I watched, entranced. My cat did too. But while I was satisfied only to look, my cat wanted more.

In moments she pounced, the monarch deposed.

I watched.

Then, my cat was distracted by passing a lizard and released the unharmed regent back into the air.

The monarch returned once more to lay her heirs.

Long live the queen.
