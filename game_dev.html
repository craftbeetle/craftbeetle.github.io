GAME DEV

I've been in love with game design ever since I first saw one of my cousins making a game when I was still a small child. Now, decades later, I have completed a few games and I continue to learn and work on my skill. Below are the games I have published. Some are solo, others were collaborative efforts with some wonderful people.

Nautilus Tank - Mini Jam #14 - 2023

A friend and I made Nautilus Tank over a weekend. He did the art and I programmed it. We had a really fun time and ended up making a neat little mini-game.

Strat-a-Dice - GMTK 2022

Strat-a-Dice was supposed to be an XCOM style game but the probability calculations were themselves random. I sure made an effort but the theme just didn't click. I finished and submitted the game but I don't even remember what was the point.

Heart Attack - GMTK 2020

Three teammates and I made Heart Attack together. My teammates made the art, music, and sound effects, while I programmed it. This game remains one of my most successful gamejam games, ranking 179/5261 or top 4%! Give it a play (it plays better when downloaded, might run too fast depending on your monitor refresh speed.)
