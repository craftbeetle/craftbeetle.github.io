Web Reflections

25.01.30 BACK TO BLOGS

This website has been a lot of fun so far. I have enjoyed learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and the creative process of designing a user experience. The most rewarding part has been the feedback I get from my friends after they visit my site and learn about the things I make, the plants and animals I see, and my thoughts on the media I enjoy.

It started out very simple and I have successfully kept it consistent and easy to manage. The content is easy to post and update and the organization and feel of the site has matured over time.

Now that the site is stabilized and matured, I think it would be interesting to experiment some more. I have fallen into a pattern, which has been immensely helpful in keeping me on track, but now I want to try new things. I also want to break out of the idea that all content has to be perfect before I post it. As I have thought, written, and created more, I have realized that the old adage that "Perfect is the enemy of good." is true here as well. Why hold back on writing my thought on a thing just because I don't think I'll perfectly express everything I want to express? These are the inhibitions of the big social media platforms, where creation is product, not art.

In short I will try to be a bit more organic and less inhibited in this site. This has never been a professional product to be sold, so I shouldn't treat it as such. It's my personal website and I invite you to continue enjoying it, flaws and all.
