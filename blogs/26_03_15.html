Al Fath, Louverture, The Opening

26.03.15 BACK TO BLOGS

We are now two weeks into the Ramadan War, also known as Operation Epstein Fury. I am sure that like me, you have been glued to your device of choice, watching the night skies of Occupied Palestine and the Traitorous Arab States streak with vengeance and liberation. At time of writing all imperial bases have been struck, many if not most have been leveled. The empire is bleeding cash, the only thing that capitalists care about, and the world is cheering for the liberation of Al-Quds. All analysis I've encountered shows that this is the final blow for the empire in the Arab region. We have played the game of biting fingers and the people simply had a tighter grip on their homes than the occupiers.

Palestinian Liberation

As the empire gets pushed further and further out, as it looses 30 years of military buildup in two weeks, as the paper tiger is set ablaze, many are looking forwards and trying to figure out what happens next. Here is one possible timeline of events.

In 1999, Sheikh Ahmed Yassin predicted that Israel wont survive past 2027. In 2015, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei predicted that nothing will survive of Israel in 25 years (2040). What will that look like?

As the empire tucks tail and runs (like to do to Cuba what Reagan did to Grenada), Israel will be left all alone in a decolonized Arab world. The military bases, free travel, and myth of security stripped away from it. The only thing left to do will be no negotiate a surrender. You may think that they are too craven and insane to surrender, but having read the Hebrew tweets and heard their panic, it is clear that their sense of strength came from the imperial core.

The terms of surrender will not be categorical exile, though it would be deserved. The Axis of Resistance is far too humane. Instead the main points will likely be:

  • Nuclear Disarmament
  • Right of Return for Palestinians
  • Withdrawal from Gaza, the West Bank, The Golan Heights
  • The Repealing of Apartheid Laws

    • But how does this make Israel cease to exist? Well, what IS Israel? Israel is fundamentally the system of apartheid in occupied Palestine. It's fundamental pillars are occupation, expulsion, extreme violence, and inequality. Take away those four pillars and the house comes crashing down.

    Israel has already admitted that 10% of it's population fled before the start of the Ramadan War. That number has likely greatly increased since they stopped reporting it. When Palestinians return to the occupied lands, with equal citizenship, they will outnumber the settlers by 8 to 7 at the very least, and they will vote to incorporate the West Bank and Gaza into a single country. That will give them an even greater electoral majority of 13 to 7. Then they will vote to rename Israel to Palestine. And just like that, Israel ceases to exist. What happens from there are the trials of War Criminals, reparations, land reforms, reeducation, and stabilization.

    Wider Arab Liberation

    With the knife in the Arab world's back fully removed and the wound beginning to heal, the foundation of colonialism will have been fully destroyed and the rest of the Arab world will also be reshaped. Now with an Iranian pillar in the east and perhaps an Egyptian pillar in the south west and a Turkish pillar in the north east. From there further decolonization will take place, and with it stabilization and unification of Arab lands.

    This is not a far fetched idea, in fact this is the natural state of the Arab world. Right up until Sykes–Picot 110 years ago. This stability and unity will reverberate across the region and the world, causing a chain reaction of liberation, stabilization, and unification.

    We may see a decolonized Arab world very soon, and it will all be thanks to Al Aqsa Flood washing away the colonizers.

    Imperial Core

    Throughout these three years of genocide and ethnic cleansing the whole planet has experienced a sharpening of the contradictions, exposing imperialism, and raising class consciousness. So many people, me included, have gone through a massive change in our politics. We have all read books, participated in protests, sat in teach-in, joined organizations, listened to analysis, cut out complicit companies, improved out operational security, and become generally better equipped but to what end?

    Our pressure and solidarity was good, but it did not liberate Al-Quds. It was only the blood of martyrs that could do that. We've rooted for the resistance, mourned the loss of our people, and sharpened our minds and bodies yet we have not had an opening to act, until now.

    Al Aqsa Flood was the beginning of Al-Fath, Louverture, The Opening. We will soon be in an empire in retreat. Political approval an all time low across the board. The Epstein class naked for all to see. The mailed fist rusted and crumbling. The belly of the beast is covered in ulcers. It will soon be our time to act.

    As the dollar hegemony is blown into the wind, we will see a drastic rise in the cost of living. Gas prices have been and will continue to increase. Our manufacturing base has long been decimated and cannot be rebuilt due to a fundamental lack in education. Immigrants who could have rebuilt it are being violently deported. We are flailing, hitting those close to US, destroying our relations with Canada and Mexico, and smashing Venezuela and Cuba. Life will only get harder from here. Our blood is in the water, the sharks are coming.

    Land Back

    As the U.S.S.R. fell so too will the US. The only difference is that we deserved it. The empire's external violence has been foiled and the imperial boomerang has returned. Fascist brown shirts roam our streets, our elite are Caligula and Crassus, our infrastructure is rotting, our people are sick and illiterate. How can this beast continue to exist with it's plundered intravenous ripped out? The little frog has been boiled, now the big toad is getting cooked.

    The only path forward is balkanization and land back. It may happen suddenly or after a century of humiliation but eventually this unnatural Frankenstein will fall into its constituent parts. Dollar hegemony will cease, the AI bubble will pop, banks and corporations will go belly up and the government wont be able to bail them out, local government will go bankrupt, border patrol will cease to function, water sources will dry up, capital will flee and with it so will the rich and their class traitor lackeys, and we will be left with a shattered land.

    What we do with that land depends on how prepared and organized we are right now. Al Aqsa flood has given us all a crash course in anti-imperialism, guerrilla warfare, steadfastness, and resistance. We must now quickly learn self governance, ecological restoration, socialism, international relations, and all the rest of what is necessary to live on the land. We must purge out the Settler Amerikanist ideology and reconnect with the Native Peoples of turtle island, not symbolically but materially, and let them lead the way. We must prepare ourselves to be free.
