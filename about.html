ABOUT ME

Name: Craft Beetle
Level: Mid 20s
Race: Sand-Brown
Class: Proletariat
Sub-Class: Artificer

Hello visitor,

I'm glad you visited my website. My goal here is to more authentically share parts of my life with the world. I have long since ditched social media, I'm not active on any of them. Instead of obsessively trying to reach a large audience, I want to create something I am proud of and share it more genuinely. I'm programming this website manually, learning HTML and CSS as I go. Surprisingly, while this process is slower and more involved, it has captured my creativity. I actually enjoy updating this website and adding to it. I know that if I don't like the layout I can just change it. I can make it exactly how I like.

I'm in love with the personal web. The beauty that people create and share using these small websites. Algorithms are designed to extract profit by causing you to develop addiction by activating the 7 deadly sins: pride, greed, wrath, envy, lust, gluttony, and sloth. If you've been scrolling through social media and asked yourself why you keep returning to it, even though it makes you feel bad, this is why. This website aims to be a respite from the modern internet's ad laden skinner boxes.

Feel free to contact me at my email below. I read every email and I tend to respond pretty quickly. I'm not seeking profit through this site, though I won't reject interesting project ideas if you have them. You can also just email me to share your thoughts, or to request a spot on my links page.

Come back again soon, I'll have more to share!

Contact me via my email: craftbeetle@protonmail.com and if you know how, use my PGP Key.
