Amerika Isn't Real

26.05.09 BACK TO BLOGS

Amerika isn't real. Never has been.

We must stop thinking of Amerika as a real thing, it isn't. When you imagine Amerika you probably imagine the continental 48, or the 50 states with Alaska and Hawaii in their little boxes. You probably don't also imagine Puerto Rico, Guam, the U.S. Virgins Islands. You almost certainly don't imagine the 800+ U.S. military bases dotted around the world (minus those vaporized by Iran and their valiant allies).

Amerika is not a real thing. It doesn't not have full control nor consent of all the territories it claims. When you imagine Amerika you probably don't imagine the Swiss cheese like First Nations Bantustans aka reservations aka concentration camps. You almost certainly don't imagine the inland archipelago of black and brown cities and neighborhoods where settlers nearly never venture save with armored trucks and intent to kill. Does that remind you of something?

Remember, Amerika is the planetary Israel. Washington D.C. is Jerusalem, seat of imperial control, surrounded by disenfranchised blacks/Palestinians. New York is Tel Aviv, capital of capital on stolen land. The west coast is the west bank, half of the occupied lands still full of indigenous First Nations/Mexicano/Palestinians. The police and ICE are the IOF, rolling through brown towns, kidnapping and killing with the aim of ethnic cleansing.

As with Israel, so with Amerika, their hold on the land is premised on the divide and conquer strategy, the perpetually broken treaties, the settlerist fear industrial complex, the violent policing of life, the homestead act, and the well funded propaganda machine. Amerikan settlers will never have a socialist revolution for the same reason Israeli settlers will never have a revolution, settlers are forever bourgeois pawns, treatlers.

We must free our minds of the settler state lie. The settler state isn't real. It will never be real. We see them falling apart as we speak, and the less control they have the more they thrash about. Perhaps it is time for Mexico to take back the stolen half of their country. Perhaps it is time for Puerto Rico/Virgin Island/Guam to declare independence. Perhaps it is time for Black Belt to break away.

Are you ready?
