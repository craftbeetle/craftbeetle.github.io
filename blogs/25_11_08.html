Cycle of Violence

25.11.08 BACK TO BLOGS

I've never heard anyone but the oppressor talk about the "Cycle of Violence". The oppressors are downright obsessed with it. It's almost like they want to convince the oppressed to stop resisting.

Well if it's a 'Cycle of Violence' did it always exist without a start point? No. Then, if someone did start the violence, who was it? If it has a start point then it can have an end point. To find out how we can arrive at that end point, let's look at those who arrived before us. Did the Algerians peacefully achieve independence and decolonization? Did the Vietnamese refrain from "Perpetuating the Cycle of Violence"? Did the Zimbabweans let the Boers visit violence upon them without responding with violence? No. The "Cycle of Violence" is code for "Let me hit you and don't hit me back."

There is no "Cycle of Violence", what there is are oppressors who will not stop oppressing until they are forced to stop at gun point.
